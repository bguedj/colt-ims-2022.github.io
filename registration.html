Please note: DALI is an invitation-only event, and it is not possible to transfer this invitation to others.

The registration fee is 450 € for attendees coming from academia and 600 € for those coming from industry. Registration includes welcome dinner, symposium and workshops, coffee breaks, and a conference dinner (possibly more).

As we are postponing DALI 2020 to October, the registration for the event is currently closed.
